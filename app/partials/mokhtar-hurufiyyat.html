Hurufiyyat (Arabic: حروفيات) is an abstract style of calligraphy that Mokhtar El-Baba developed in recent years. The idea behind this innovative style is to present a blend of Arabic letters ("Huruf" حروف in Arabic) that do not form recognizable words, yet conform closely to the rules of classic calligraphy. The result is a genuine style of calligraphy that is true to the classical art, yet one that does not require the knowledge of the Arabic language to be fully appreciated.
